# Cookie Policy

We use cookies in a reasonable way. Layerly respects your device and privacy. We do not use aggressive third-party marketing cookies to sell your data to ad systems.

## What types of cookies do we use?

### Essential (required)

Without these, the Layerly app would not work. They store your session token (so you don't have to log in on every click), basket settings and security preferences (e.g. CSRF tokens). They cannot be disabled.

### Analytics and performance

They help us understand how you use the app (e.g. which actions are used most, which cause errors). We use anonymised data where possible. You can disable these in privacy settings where offered.

## Managing cookies

You can change your cookie preferences in your browser at any time. Blocking all cookies may prevent you from logging into Layerly.
