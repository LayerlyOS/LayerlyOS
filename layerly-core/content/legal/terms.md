# Terms of Service (SaaS)

These terms define the rules for providing services electronically by Layerly Cloud to business (B2B) and consumer clients, as part of the operating system for 3D print farms.

- **Software / System:** Layerly web application available as SaaS (Software as a Service), used for automatic 3D model quoting, machine fleet management and generating offers.

## 2. Conclusion of the agreement and subscription

Use of the System requires creating an Account and choosing one of the available subscription plans. The service agreement is concluded when the first subscription payment is recorded. Layerly guarantees system availability (Uptime) at **99.9% per month**. If this level is not met, the User is entitled to a proportional refund in the form of credits for subsequent months.

Given the B2B nature of the service, the Service Provider's total liability for non-performance or improper performance is limited to the amount corresponding to the sum of subscription fees paid by the User in the 12 months preceding the event.

## 5. Termination and data deletion

The User may delete their Account at any time. Account deletion results in permanent and irreversible deletion of all uploaded 3D files, quotes and CRM data from Layerly servers within 30 days (in line with backup cycles).
