# Privacy Policy (GDPR)

Your privacy and the security of your data are our top priorities. This document explains in plain language what data we collect, why we do it, and who we share it with.

## 1. Data controller

The controller of your personal data is **Layerly** (or the entity operating Layerly). For data protection matters you can contact us at [privacy@layerly.cloud](mailto:privacy@layerly.cloud).

## 2. What data we process and why

- **Performance of the service (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR):** We process your email, name and company details (tax ID, address) to create your Account, issue invoices and provide access to the calculator.
- **Payment processing:** Card data is processed directly by a certified payment provider (e.g. Stripe). Layerly does not store full card numbers (PCI-DSS compliance).
- **Technical support (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR):** We process login history and system errors to resolve issues you report.

## Sub-processors (who we share data with)

> To deliver a reliable system we use trusted cloud providers with signed data processing agreements (DPA):
>
> - **Hosting** – Servers and 3D file storage (within the EU where applicable).
> - **Stripe Inc.** – Subscription and payment processing.
> - **Email provider** – System emails and notifications.

## 3. Your rights (GDPR)

Under European law you have full control over your data. You have the right to:

- Access your data and receive a copy.
- Rectify incorrect data.
- **Request erasure ("right to be forgotten")** – you can do this from your Settings.
- Restrict processing and to data portability.
- Lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority.
